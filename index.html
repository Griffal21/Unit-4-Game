Mysterious Adding Game!



You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four mathematicians below to help you. By clicking on a mathematician's button you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by batching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.


Now, the mathematicians are reluctant to help with with matters they deem trivial. As a result they have hidden the value they add to the total score


Purely for the reason of making things difficult each time when the game starts, the mathematicians will change the value of their button.
